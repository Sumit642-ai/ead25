ABOUT THIS EVENT

Empowering the Future

The PAN India Drive, showcasing our flagship EAD LSM event, energizes 30+ cities to ignite student entrepreneurship and fortify local startup ecosystems. Dive into workshops uncovering entrepreneurship secrets, absorb insights from industry leaders through dynamic panel discussions, and connect with investors and founders for tailored mentorship and strategic networking opportunities.
